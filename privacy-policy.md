# Privacy Policy for Circle Strike

**Last updated: April 26, 2026**

Jelly Game Studios ("we", "us", "our") operates the mobile game **Circle Strike** (the "Game"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Game, you agree to the practices described below.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account information (only when you sign in)
The Game can be played fully offline. If you choose to sign in for the world leaderboard or to back up your progress, we receive the following from Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple:
- Your email address
- Your name (or chosen display name)
- A unique account identifier (Firebase UID)
- Profile picture URL (Google only)

You can play the Game without signing in. If you do not sign in, no account information is collected.

### 1.2 Game progress and gameplay data
We store the following on your device and, if you sign in, sync it to our servers:
- Coins, gems, level, XP, and unlocked cosmetics
- Game session stats (best score, total games played)
- Achievement progress
- Purchase history within the Game (which packs / passes you bought)
- Your customized in-game character ("Jel") name and equipped items

### 1.3 Leaderboard data (public)
If you sign in and submit a score, your **display name and high score** become visible to other players on the global leaderboard. We do not display your email or full name on the leaderboard.

### 1.4 Purchase data
When you make an in-app purchase:
- Google Play Billing processes the transaction and shares the purchase token with us
- RevenueCat (our subscription management partner) records the purchase to honor it across devices and reinstalls
- We do **not** receive or store your payment card details. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google.

### 1.5 Technical data (collected automatically)
- Device model, operating system version, screen size
- Crash reports and error logs (Firebase Crashlytics, if enabled)
- IP address (collected by Firebase for security and abuse prevention)
- Anonymous identifiers used by Firebase App Check to verify the app is legitimate
- Aggregated, non-personal app usage events via **Firebase Analytics** (e.g., screen views, session duration). These events do not include personally identifying information and are used only to understand which features are used and how to improve the Game.

### 1.6 No advertising or behavioral profiling
Circle Strike does **not** display advertisements, integrate any ad SDK, or share data with advertising networks. We do not build advertising profiles of you, sell or rent your information to advertisers, or use your data for any form of behavioral targeting.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data only to:
1. Operate and maintain the Game's features (saving progress, syncing across devices, world leaderboard)
2. Process in-app purchases and restore them on reinstall
3. Detect fraud, abuse, and cheating (e.g., manipulated scores)
4. Diagnose bugs and improve the Game
5. Communicate critical updates if you've signed in (very rarely; we do not send marketing emails)

We do **not** sell your personal information. We do **not** use your data for advertising or behavioral profiling.

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

- Local game progress is stored encrypted on your device using industry-standard AES-256 encryption.
- Cloud-synced data is stored in Google Firebase servers (regions: United States and European Union).
- We retain account data for as long as your account exists. If you stop using the Game, you may request deletion at any time (see Section 7).

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

Circle Strike is rated for **ages 13 and up**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. Parents who believe their child has provided us information may contact us at the email below.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR for EU/UK, CCPA for California), you have the right to:
- **Access** the personal information we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate information
- **Delete** your account and associated data
- **Export** your data in a portable format
- **Object** to certain processing
- **Withdraw consent** at any time by signing out and uninstalling the Game

To exercise any of these rights, email us at the address below. We respond within 30 days.

---

## 7. Account Deletion

You can delete your Circle Strike account at any time by:
1. Emailing **jellygamesstudios@gmail.com** with the subject line "Delete my account" and the email associated with your sign-in, **OR**
2. Using the "Delete Account" option in the in-game Settings screen (where available)

When you delete your account, we erase your Firebase profile, leaderboard entries, cloud-synced progress, and RevenueCat record within 30 days. Some data may be retained longer if required by law (e.g., transaction records for tax or fraud-prevention purposes).

---

## 8. Security

We use the following safeguards:
- AES-256 encryption for all locally stored data
- TLS 1.2+ for all network communication
- Firebase App Check to verify requests come from a legitimate app instance
- Server-side validation of in-app purchases via RevenueCat
- No storage of payment credentials on our systems

No system is perfectly secure. If we ever experience a data breach affecting your information, we will notify affected users within 72 hours where required by law.
