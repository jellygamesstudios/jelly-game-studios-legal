# Account Deletion — Circle Strike

**Last updated: April 26, 2026**

This page explains how to delete your **Circle Strike** account and the data associated with it.

---

## What gets deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data is permanently erased within **30 days**:

- Your Firebase user account (email, display name, profile picture URL)
- Your unique account identifier (UID)
- Cloud-synced game progress (coins, gems, level, XP, achievements, owned cosmetics)
- Your leaderboard entries (display name + scores)
- RevenueCat purchase records linked to your account
- Any in-game character ("Jel") customizations stored in the cloud

## What is retained (and why)

The following data may be retained for limited periods to comply with legal or accounting obligations:

- **Transaction records** — retained up to 7 years for tax, audit, and fraud-prevention purposes (Google Play Billing transaction logs). These contain only the transaction ID, amount, and timestamp — never personal data.
- **Anonymized aggregate analytics** — e.g., "X total gem packs were purchased in March 2026." Cannot be linked back to you.

## How to request deletion

Choose any of the methods below:

### Method 1: Email (recommended)

Send an email to **jellygamesstudios@gmail.com** with:
- **Subject line:** `Delete my Circle Strike account`
- **Body:** Include the email address you used to sign in (Google or Apple). This is the only information we need to identify your account.

We confirm receipt within 3 business days and complete deletion within 30 days. You will receive an email confirmation when the deletion is complete.

### Method 2: In-app

If you have the latest version of the Game installed:
1. Open Circle Strike
2. Tap **Settings** (gear icon, top right of the home screen)
3. Tap **Account → Delete Account**
4. Confirm the action when prompted

The in-app option triggers an immediate request and you will receive email confirmation when deletion is complete.

## Frequently asked questions

**Q: Can I recover my account or progress after deletion?**
A: No. Deletion is permanent and irreversible. We recommend signing into a different device first if you want to keep your progress before deleting.

**Q: Will my purchases be refunded?**
A: Account deletion does not entitle you to a refund. To request a refund for a recent purchase, please contact Google Play Support directly. Past in-app purchases (e.g., gems already spent) cannot be refunded once consumed.

**Q: I never signed in. Do I still have an account?**
A: No. If you played offline only and never signed in with Google or Apple, no account or cloud data exists. Uninstalling the app will erase all locally stored data on your device.

**Q: How do I just stop the Game from collecting data without deleting?**
A: Sign out of your account in **Settings → Account → Sign out**. Once signed out, no further account or cloud data is collected. To stop technical data collection (anonymous analytics), uninstall the Game.
